The world measures runners by
how fast they go.
We measure them by
whether they showed up.

Not your split time. Not your VO2 max. Not how many followers watched your finish line photo.

Showing up is the hardest part. Getting out of bed when it's dark. Choosing the run over the couch. Being there for your crew on a cold Wednesday when nobody would blame you for staying home.

Show up for yourself. For your club. For the person next to you who's faster and the one who's slower. None of it counts if you're not there.

HSTL makes showing up count.

What is HSTL

The consistency layer
for run clubs

HSTL sits alongside the tools you already use. Strava tracks your training. WhatsApp coordinates your group. Instagram tells your story. HSTL does the one thing none of them can: verify who actually showed up, and make that matter.

Verified attendance

GPS check-in at the run. Optional Strava proof. No guessing, no spreadsheets, no "I was there, I swear."

Streaks and consistency

Active weeks. Current streaks. The kind of recognition that rewards commitment, not just speed.

Club pulse

Who's your core? Who's drifting? Turnout trends, returning runners, the health of your community — visible at a glance.

Who it's for

Runners

  • Build a streak that means something
  • Get recognised for consistency, not pace

Club organisers

  • Data that proves your club's value — to brands and sponsors
How it works

Simple. By design.

1

Join your club

Find your run club in the directory, or ask your organiser to list it. One tap to join.

2

Show up

See upcoming runs, tap "I'm in", and get there. That's the hard part. We know.

3

Check in

GPS check-in at the run location. One tap. Your attendance is verified, your streak lives on.

4

Watch it build

Active weeks. Streaks. Milestones. Leaderboards based on consistency. Your club career, building week by week.
