Our Ethos

Built for Community, Not Competition

We don't care how fast you are. We care that you showed up.

1

ETHOS

Lace Up & Show Up

The hardest part of any run is the first step out the door. We celebrate the habit of being at the start line, rain or shine.

2

COMMUNITY

Like Minded Mates

Running is the vehicle, but community is the destination. We prioritize real-world attendance over digital vanity metrics.

3

CONSISTENCY

Stack the Streaks

Forget PBs. Your best metric is how many weeks in a row you turned up. Consistency compounds — in fitness, in friendships, in everything.

For Runners

Simple. By design.

Four steps to building your verified record of consistency.

Find your club

Browse the directory or ask your organiser to list their club. One tap to join.

Show up

See upcoming runs, tap "I'm in", and get there. That's the hard part. We know.

Check in

GPS check-in at the run. One tap. Your attendance is verified, your streak lives on.

Watch it build

Active weeks. Streaks. Milestones. A leaderboard built on consistency. Your club career, growing week by week.
